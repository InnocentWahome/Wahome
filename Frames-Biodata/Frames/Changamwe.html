Changamwe District

Changamwe is a suburb of Mombasa, in Mombasa County, in the former Coast Province of Kenya. It is primarily industrial with a number of modern concrete tower blocks housing residents. Industries include refineries and various process industries. There is a franchise of Barclays Bank located on the major Mombasa-Nairobi road.


Some of the products manufuctured here are:

  • Cement
  • Refined cooking oils
  • African wears: Kitenges
  • Leather products
